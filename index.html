AUTOCLAVE

1. Vertical Autoclave

Usage: Laboratory, Clinics, and small healthcare centers.

Mechanism: Steam under pressure (Gravity displacement).

  • Top-loading cylindrical chamber.
  • Stainless steel construction.

2. Horizontal Autoclave

Usage: Large Hospitals, CSSD, and O.T.

Mechanism: Pre-vacuum steam sterilization.

  • Front-loading rectangular chamber.
  • High capacity for bulk sets and linens.
